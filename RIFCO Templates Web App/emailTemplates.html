Subject Line – Online Banking Payment Instructions - RIFCO

Western Union Instructions

Subject Line – Western Union Payment Instructions - RIFCO

As requested, please find instructions on how to complete a Western Union payment below:

• Pick up a blue “Quick Collect” form
• Under “Pay To” write: Rifco
• Under “Code City” write: Rifco
• Under “Province” write: AB
• Under “Account Number” write your account number
• Under “Amount” write the amount of the discussed payment

Once a payment has successfully been made, please contact our office back toll free at 1-844-314-1214 and report the payment made with the Money Transfer Control Number (MTCN) provided.

This information is also available online at our website www.rifco.net under the “Make a Payment” tab.
