SERVICES

Software and CNC Integration Solutions for the Woodworking Industry, specializing in Cabinet Vision and Mozaik and Weeke CNC's, while servicing all software / machine combinations.


We're primarily focused on providing three services -


*Complete Software Setup and CNC Integration Package

A turnkey contract service offering complete software setup, complete integration with your CNC machine and hands-on training, giving you and your staff the ability to quickly and efficiently generate machine tested ready to cut files for your CNC machine control.

Full setup and integration package includes - A complete from scratch set up of the customers software and full integration with the customers CNC machining centre, a fully expandable setup precisely tailored to customer specifications for construction and assembly methods, materials and hardware, making every attempt at a seamless integration with existing methods and systems.

Software and CNC Training - We provide on site and hands on training for both CNC programmers and operators, taking them right from establishing parameter sets through to ready-to-run code at the machine, and we provide full tech support in the period following the integration process.

We do this by working closely with the customer to layout the current or proposed systems and methods, the future manufacturing goals, and the equipment intended for use.

Using this data and the customers choice of software/CNC, we design engineer and implement a fully expanadble Lean Nested Based "System" for programing / cutting / machining and production.

Our purpose is to leave in place a "System" of Lean computerized manufacturing that can easily and efficiently be modified by the user, one that the user fully understands how to exploit for maximum benefit.

With any software and machine combination you have to get them working together to produce exactly what you want, and getting the software to do what you want is always the issue. Once you're there you need the ability get that code to your CNC as quickly and efficiently as possible - we show you how to do all that, from start to finish.


*Off Site Programming

Contract based programing for the millwork industry and very straightforward, you send us your architectural spec and drawings, your approved shops and your site dimensions - and we send you the ready to cut files, a set of working drawings for the production line and a bill of the materials required.

We do this by visiting your facility and making a copy of your machine control, including the machine tool database and post processor so we're able to send you machine files in the exact same format your own staff uses. This allows you to run our files seamlessly and concurrently with your own programming work without making any tooling or machine parameter changes.

We can also program any specialty item or component you can imagine, if you're ever stuck for programming solutions please contact us.


*Manufacturing Solutions for Lean Nested Based CNC Production.

A service provided on a consultation basis, for new and existing woodworking manufacturers who want to implement the latest technology in Lean Nested Based manufacturing. From one-off custom and specialty work through to high production fixtures, displays and architectural millwork.

Support and Integrated Solutions for everything from your existing software and CNC equipment, to new plant equipment requirements and layout, production, construction, assembly and finishing methods and systems.

My Work Experience

I started training as cabinetmaker at Cassin Remco on Norfinch Dr in Toronto some 40 years ago and I've worked full time in the Canadian millwork industry since then.

I've made everything from production, reproduction and custom furniture, architectural millwork, store fixtures, displays, exhibits, custom stairs-doors-windows, to mahogany runabouts, stringed instruments, wheel cut glass, turning/milling/machining/welding stainless steel, all sorts of ornamental metal work and some jewelry.

I've worked with lumber solids, veneers and sheet stock of every description, laminates/plastics and acrylics/phenolics/celluloids/shagreen/bone and baleen, all sorts of glass, brass/aluminum/copper/stainless steel and precious metals.

I worked for a number of Toronto cabinet shops prior to starting a family woodworking business with a retail outlet on Eglinton Ave in 1983. This evolved into businesses with numerous specialties - from furniture into store fixtures, displays, exhibits through to architectural millwork of every description right into 2002.

Prior to retiring from full time work in April of this year, I spent the last 17 years general managing a Toronto millwork shop specializing in commercial / institutional millwork, and was I fortunate to have spent the last 7 + years working alongside my son Darren.

In my 40 years of working I've been directly involved both for myself and as a consultant, in the layout and setup of numerous woodworking manufacturing facilities, and multiple setups and integration of third party software with CNC machines.

I sat on the AWMAC Ontario Board in 2009 - 2010 and I'm currently still AWMAC certified for work on GIS projects based on the latest North American AWS edition 3.1



My CNC and CAD Experience

My first exposure to CNC woodworking machinery was programming (right at the machine) and operating an SCM beam saw, and programing a Biesse pod and rail CNC with an AutoCad generated parts library and an add-on called SmartLister, in the late 80's.

At the time there was a lot of code editing done at the machine control to get everything just right, today the software is light years ahead. We're now multiple generations into third party software like Cabinet Vision and Mozaik that once fully exploited bring significant advantages to the user.

I've spent more than 17 years full time engineering projects and programing components, 10's of thousands of cabinets, displays and fixtures, and thousands of specialty item/components of in all sorts of materials.

I've been using Planit Solutions Cabnetware (since V5.0), Planit's Cabinet Vision (since V9.0) and Weeke's WoodWop (since V4.0), Mozaik (since v5.0), with AlphaCam and Autocad, I've also worked with Biesse, SCM and Thermwood's own software.

With this new business we plan to support integration using Microvellum in the near future.
