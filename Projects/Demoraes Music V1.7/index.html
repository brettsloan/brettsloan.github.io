Lessons

Are you just starting out in music? Looking to improve your skills in specific areas? I cover everything from the basics to more advanced lessons.

I teach guitar and drums for both beginners and more intermediate players looking to better their skills.

Lessons are $30 each, once a week for half an hour (excluding holidays) and are paid as one monthly fee on the first lesson on the month.

About Me

My name is Darren Demoraes and music has always been a huge part of my life. My grandfather was a great jazz drummer and he was the one who inspired me to start playing drums at an early age.

I got my first drum set when I was 8 years old. From there I studied drumming under John Hughes at Georgina Music. I took drum lessons for 6 years and still continue to play to this day.

I received my first guitar at 16 and took only a handful of lessons. I was old enough then to teach myself everything I know now.

I've played multiple instruments in many bands covering all sorts of genres. I still play guitar and drums every day and continue to learn new things while bettering myself anyway that I can.
